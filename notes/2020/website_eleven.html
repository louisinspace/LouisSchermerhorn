My site is live!

Written July 9, 2020

Website

A few minutes ago, I updated my DNS settings to point my GitHub pages site to lschermerhorn.com, so my website is officially live!

Before settling on this URL, there were a few other options I considered:

  1. louisschermerhorn.com
  2. louisbschermerhorn.com
  3. louisinspace.com

Number 1 is the most straightforward, but I do not like the way that the two names appear when there isn't a break. And I didn't want to put a hyphen in the URL. Number 2 is a possible solution for that, but I don't really use my middle name often, anywhere online. Wouldn't be consistent with other places that people might find me like LinkedIn or Instagram. Number 3 is my online profile name, something I really like for its simplicity and the way it allows me to use my first name. But this being a professional site, I thought that would be too confusing.

Welcome to www.lschermerhorn.com!
