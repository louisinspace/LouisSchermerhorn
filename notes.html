Notes


Welcome to the organizing structure for my random opinions and journal entries. I don't intend to keep this regularly updated, but every so often I have an experience that I think is worth documenting. Not just what happened, but what I thought about it, how I felt. I do this mostly for myself. The hope is that there are some of you out there who are like me (or perhaps nothing like me) who find something nice in my writing about various topics.

I like playing video games, watching movies, seeing theatre, working on random creative projects and thinking about technology. You might find some thoughts related to those topics in the list below.

My small stint in filmmaking

Written June 19, 2020    Creative

I have always been fascinated by the stories of how entertainment gets made and sold. I remember watching the behind-the-scenes videos that preceded the Star Wars Special Edition VHS releases and being mesmerized. These people were artists, problem-solvers, engineers. And they were playing, actually *playing!* with miniatures and models and rigs. It appeared to be more fun than watching the movies.
